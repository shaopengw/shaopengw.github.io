简介About

助理教授，硕士研究生导师。西南财经大学管理学博士，曾受国家留学基金委（CSC）资助在苏黎世联邦理工学院（ETH Zurich）计算机系联合培养（2022–2023）。从事金融科技、商务智能、人工智能和多模态大语言模型相关研究。在 IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering、Information Sciences、Neural Networks 和《计算机研究与发展》等国内外高水平期刊发表论文 7 篇。担任 IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering、IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems、ACM SIGKDD、AAAI、ACM MM 等国际顶级期刊和会议审稿人。作为第一主笔人撰写的研究报告 3 次获省部级领导批示。主持并完成厅级项目2项，参与江苏省社科项目 1 项。

Shaopeng Wei received his Ph.D. from Southwestern University of Finance and Economics in 2024. He was a visiting scholar at ETH Zurich (2022–2023) funded by CSC. Currently an assistant professor at Guangxi University. His research interests include graph learning, multi-agent systems, and fintech.
