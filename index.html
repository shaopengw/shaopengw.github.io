魏少朋 Shaopeng Wei

简介About Me

魏少朋，助理教授，硕士研究生导师。西南财经大学管理学博士，曾受到国家留学基金委（CSC）资助在苏黎世联邦理工学院（ETH Zurich）计算机系以联合培养博士研究生身份学习（2022-2023）。从事金融科技、商务智能、人工智能和多模态大语言模型相关研究。在IEEE TKDE，Information Sciences，Neural Networks和计算机研究与发展等国内外高水平期刊上发表研究成果6篇。担任IEEE TKDE、IEEE TNNLS、ACM SIGKDD、AAAI、ACM MM、Information Sciences、Expert Systems With Applications等国际顶级期刊和会议的审稿人。作为第一主笔人撰写的研究报告3次获得省部级党委/政府（主要）领导批示。主持厅级项目一项，参与江苏社科项目一项。

Shaopeng Wei received his B.S. degree from Huazhong Agricultural University in 2019, and Ph.D. degree from Southwestern University of Finance and Economics in 2024. He was a visiting scholar at ETH Zurich. He is currently an assistant professor at Guangxi University.His research interests include graph learning and its applications in Fintech. He has published papers in top journals such as IEEE TKDE, Information Sciences.
